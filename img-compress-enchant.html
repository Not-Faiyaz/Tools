How to Optimize Your Images

Compression Tips

  • For web images, 60-80% quality usually provides the best balance
  • Resize images to their display dimensions before uploading
  • Convert PNG to JPG when transparency isn't needed for smaller files

Enhancement Tips

  • Use moderate sharpening (30-60%) for best results
  • Adjust brightness/contrast in small increments (5-15%)
  • Try the Auto Enhance feature first, then fine-tune manually
